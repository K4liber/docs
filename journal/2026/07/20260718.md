Coming back from EuroPython 2026. The community is very friendly, welcoming and inclusive.

The most thought-provoking moment for me was a lunch talk with a person (I don't remember the name, it's not relevant) working for ICEYE. The company is building, among other things, systems for global militaries. The talk made me ask myself if I could work on such systems. Our society is very complex and part of what makes it strong and resilient is having a strong military.

Keeping myself strong and healthy, which I try to do, makes me more capable and resilient. Being close to such "power", I have more control over it and can use it for good, which I hope I do. But on a society level (or a country level), the control over such power is much further away from me. The dilemma is not only relevant to ICEYE. All powerful systems (military, aggregate intelligence, nuclear weapons, etc.) are a double-edged sword. At this point, all I can do is trust the company (or the government) will only use that power for "good" (e.g. defense over oppression).

To chill the atmosphere a bit, I think nobody should carry the weight of the world on their own shoulders. Let's stay curious, humble and kind. And let's not forget to have fun and enjoy life.
